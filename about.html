About Us

Company Profile

ZL Photonics is a leading designer and manufacturer of optoelectronic products, dedicated to providing core optoelectronic solutions for fields such as data centers, artificial intelligence, mobile communications, and 3D sensing.

Who We Are

We focus on photonics technologies and optoelectronic product development for high-performance communication and sensing applications. Our work is centered on practical device innovation, product design, and scalable solutions for fast-growing markets.

With a strong engineering orientation, ZL Photonics aims to support next-generation infrastructure with reliable optoelectronic building blocks and application-focused product development.

Focus Areas

  • Data center optics
  • Artificial intelligence infrastructure
  • Mobile communication applications
  • 3D sensing technologies
  • Core optoelectronic solutions
